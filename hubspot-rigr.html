How pricing works

Rigr Centrum™ is a monthly subscription program, and pricing starts at $49/month (or roughly $1.63 per day). Your membership plan includes a month’s supply of precisely personalized daily supplement packs delivered right to your door every 30-days, plus access to customized content based on your health and wellness goals.

We also have a 100% worry free money-back guarantee if you’re not thrilled with Rigr Centrum™ (but we know you will be).

Removing supplements

We’re confident we’ve chosen the right supplements for you, but we know sometimes you may want to make some changes. You can remove supplements from your recommended plan if it suits your needs. However, you’ll need to keep a minimum of three supplements in your plan.
